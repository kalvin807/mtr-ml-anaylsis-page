Train delay analysis driven by machine learning

Joint project between MTR and HKU

Introduction

Improve the one of the most busiest metro network

Hong Kong has one of the busiest metro networks in the world, serving over 5 million passengers every day. This extensive metro is also the backbone of Hong Kong transportation that delays and accidents may halt many million citizen activities. MTR company is looking to utilize its high-volume historical data to create an intelligent model to provide a resilient and reliable service for Hong Kong citizen.

Analyse high volume metro data using machine learning

We aim to develop a tool driven by machine learning, to predict the processing time of delays taking place along the Kwun Tong line. This prediction model will help MTR make a prompt and appropriate response to incidents and delays.
